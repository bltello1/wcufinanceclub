Our mission
More than a club — a career launchpad.
The WCU Finance Club equips students with hands-on skills, industry connections, and the confidence to succeed in banking, investments, and beyond. Whether you're a finance major or just finance-curious, there's a place for you here.
What we offer
Skills that matter in the real world.
Every session is designed to give you something tangible — a skill sharpened, a connection made, or a career question answered.
Microsoft Excel training
Master financial modeling, pivot tables, and the Excel tools used daily in finance careers.
Investment fundamentals
Learn how markets work, how to evaluate stocks, and how to think like a portfolio manager.
Banking & financial planning
Deep dives into banking operations, personal finance strategy, and the financial advisory world.
Mock interviews
Practice finance interview questions in a low-stakes setting and get honest, useful feedback.
Resume workshops
Craft a resume that stands out to finance recruiters, with targeted feedback and examples.
Speaker sessions
Hear directly from finance professionals — bankers, analysts, advisors — on their real-world experiences.
